Anne Caroll Moore

Children's Librarian Extraordinaire

Fast Facts

  • b. 1871
  • wanted to be a lawyer but didn't have the family/financial support
  • studied at the Pratt Institute library school in Brooklyn
  • in charge of one of the first library children's rooms in the country
  • advocated for quality, not just didactic, children's literature
  • famous for her scathing review of Stuart Little
